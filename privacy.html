Myrin Institute Websit Privacy Policy

Myrin.org respects the privacy concerns of visitors to our website and the importance of protecting the privacy of any information submitted by anyone who interacts with our site. As a general policy no personal information such as a visitor's name, address, e-mail address, or telephone number, is automatically collected on our website. Such information is collected only when knowingly and voluntarily submitted to us via our contact options. We maintain reasonable and appropriate safeguards to ensure the security, integrity and privacy of such information in our possession. We use personal information only for the purpose for which it was submitted. Such as, we may use personal contact information you submit to this site to send you information about us that was requested. If you supply us with an email address and/or other information, we adhere to a strict policy that respects your privacy. Such information is accepted via phone or by email from our website and we may use that information to inform you of related information. We do not rent or sell email addresses or any other information to brokers or any other third party. Information requested may include contact information such as name, address, phone or email address. If you feel that we are not abiding by this privacy policy, please contact us immediately.
